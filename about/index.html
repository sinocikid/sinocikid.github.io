About me

🔐 Cybersecurity is my passion 🌟! I completed a degree in Information System Security at SAIT, where I delved into 🛡️ security technologies, practices, and standards. I also studied legal 📜 and ethical codes of conduct in the field.

👨‍⚕️ With a strong background in medicine, I served as a general surgeon at Baotou Tumor Hospital for over four years. I’m fascinated by the intersection 🚦 of healthcare and cybersecurity.

🚀 I’ve honed my skills through various projects and challenges, like Rea Team, 🕵️‍♂️ penetration testing, and CTFs (Capture The Flag). I’ve also earned multiple security certifications 🎖️, including CompTIA A+, Security+, Network+, Cloud+, Pentest+, Microsoft SC-900, AZ-104, AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, and CAMS. Proudly, I achieved a top 1% rank 🏆 on TryHackMe, a platform for learning and practicing cybersecurity.

📚 I stay informed on the latest industry developments by attending conferences 🎫, participating in online communities, and reading publications and blogs. My goal? To pursue a career in cybersecurity and bolster the security and resilience of organizations and systems.

🤝 I love learning new things and connecting with people across various industries. Feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect.
